# Quality Audit Template

Use this template before launch to honestly assess your book from a buyer's perspective.

---

## Scoring rubric

For each chapter, score 1-5 on:

| Criterion | What a 5 means | What a 1 means |
|-----------|---------------|----------------|
| **Specificity** | "I can picture the exact scene" | "This could be in any blog post" |
| **Vulnerability** | "Genuinely uncomfortable honesty" | "LinkedIn humble-brag" |
| **Actionability** | "I have a concrete next step" | "Interesting story, now what?" |
| **Depth** | "This is worth paying for" | "I've seen this advice for free" |
| **Voice** | "I can hear the author" | "This sounds generic" |

## The refund test

If someone read only the first 3 chapters and then decided whether to keep reading or ask for a refund, what would happen? Is the opening strong enough to earn the rest of the book?

## Generic prose check

Scan for passages that sound flat or formulaic:
- Overly balanced "on the one hand... on the other hand" constructions
- Lists of three with identical grammatical patterns
- "It's worth noting that..." or "Importantly,..."
- Paragraphs that summarise what was just said
- Unnaturally smooth transitions
- Sentences that sound like conclusions without preceding arguments

## Repetition check

List any stories, lessons, or phrases that appear in multiple chapters without cross-referencing.
