## A note for beta readers

Thank you for reading an early version of this book. Your feedback is invaluable.

As you read, I'd love to know:

- Which chapters feel slow, repetitive, or padded?
- Which stories or examples don't land?
- Is there anything missing that you expected to find?
- Any typos, grammatical errors, or awkward phrasing?
- Which parts made you think "this is why I'm reading this book"?

You can send feedback however works for you — email, a marked-up document, bullet points, voice notes. Anything helps.

Thank you.
